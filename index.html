Our Story

Born from the Barrel, Built for the Table.

Double Barrel PH started with one obsession: what happens when you give smoked meat the time it deserves? No shortcuts, no sauce to mask it. Just hardwood, heat, and hours of commitment.

We craft every rack and every belly slab with the same principle — if you wouldn't be proud to serve it at your table, it doesn't leave ours. Premium quality, unapologetically bold, made for Filipinos who know real barbecue.
